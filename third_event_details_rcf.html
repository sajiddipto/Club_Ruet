Career Talk 2018

The most high-reaching aspiring event of the webinar series ‘Career Talk’ presented by RUET Career Forum has finally successfully ended its first season this month. We were fortunate to have a wide range of experts from higher education sites, renewable companies, business and from other professional fields in this webinar series. Since we are entering a new growth phase in the corporate world, advices, guidelines and comments from all the honorable speakers were very valuable and path directing for us. How they presented their experience, insights and career advice were both informative and practical and have inspired all to come up with some exciting & challenging ideas.
