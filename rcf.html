About RCF

RUET Career Forum is a student organization. Its aim is to work for the career development of RUET students.

RCF aims to make students competent for the professional world.

Our activities :
1. Regular sessions for skills development
2. Developing English, Computer and Presentation skills
3. Regular Practice sessions for developing various skills
4. Preparation for various jobs
5. Regular update for various job circulars, competitions
6. Arranging career related workshops, seminars, competitions
7. Any other activities related to career development
