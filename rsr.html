About RSR

Robotic Society of RUET (RSR) is a place for robotics and automation students, enthusiasts and hobbyists from RUET. It facilitates collaboration, skill sharing and learning among the members focusing both professional skill development and competitive programs organized around the country and abroad. Robotic Society of RUET is the focal point for activities and projects related to robotics in the campus, with its reach expanding steadily each year. In May, 2006, Robotic Society of RUET (RSR) raised its sail to the island of technology. With no huge investment but with a view to building a paragon of Electra-mechanics, it started its voyage. It opened its innings to play a tremendous knock in the long run. It is a non-political educational club, mostly related to science and technology. It is the club of science lovers, by science lovers, for science lovers. Actually, it is the consequence of realization of liabilities of engineers towards the society . It was established to change the usual flow of our non-practical, non-creative moods of education. It started with hopes to burn our brain with technical energy.
